Oversized proof-point stat — the brand leads with evidence. Use in a row/grid as an "impact band".

```jsx
<StatBlock value="$94.8B" label="Economic impact since 1983" />
<StatBlock value="18,992" label="Jobs created" tone="red" />
<StatBlock value="72,000+" label="Students supported" tone="dark" />
```

Tones: `light` (number on white), `red`, `dark`, `gold`. The label sits under a heavy colored rule. Keep labels short and factual.
